## Design Context

### Users
Doctors, clinics, D2C founders, entrepreneurs, and growth-minded brands who need a digital identity that turns online attention into recognition, trust, and business inquiries.

### Brand Personality
Clear, memorable, and commercially sharp. Pehechan should feel confident without being loud, strategic without becoming corporate, and human enough for medical and founder-led businesses.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use the Influencely reference as the visual base: pale lavender canvas, large rounded sans headlines, pill navigation, generous editorial spacing, image-led sections, lavender-to-blue gradient buttons and counters, dark radial proof section, card reveal motion, and restrained page-load/scroll reveals.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with identity and recognition, not generic marketing claims.
2. Keep sections spacious, visual, and easy to scan.
3. Use motion to reveal priority content, not as decoration.
4. Make doctors, founders, and brands visible in the copy so the page has clear SEO relevance.
5. Preserve the reference template's typography scale, image cards, partner strip, dark proof band, progress bars, and ripple interaction language.
